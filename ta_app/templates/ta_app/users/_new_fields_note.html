  • For graduate students only, Total number of academic years, Total number of terms and Total number of TA hours

Examples for 3 GTA Totals

Total number of academic years worked as a GTA: 2021/22 + 2022/23 + 2023/24 = 3 academic years.

Total number of Terms worked as a GTA: 2021/22 W1 and 2021/22 W2 + 2022/23 W1 + 2023/24 W2 = 4 terms.

Total number of GTA hours worked: TAships for APBI 200 001 of 100 GTA h + FRST 444 001 of 96 GTA h + CHEM 101 001 (U of Guelph) of 200 GTA h = 396 Total GTA hours.
